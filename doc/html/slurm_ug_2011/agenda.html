Agenda

The 2011 SLURM User Group Meeting will be held on September 22 and 23 in Phoenix, Arizona and will be hosted by Bull.

September 22: Tutorials.
There will be two parallel tracks of tutorials meeting in separate rooms. One set of tutorials will be for users and the other will be for system adminitrators.
